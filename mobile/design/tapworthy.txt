Chapter 1: Touch and Go
-----------------------
> most people use mobile apps on the go which means they need large designs
  and text to see the screen properly
> distractions are the norm when using apps - not just real world distractions
  but on-device distractions as well, such as push notifications, text
  messages, and emails
> the best apps have a focused job description
> choose an idea, focus, do the minimum, and polish it many times
> only a third of downloaded apps get used at all after two months
> many users do not even discover that apps have a landscape view
